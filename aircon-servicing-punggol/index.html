About Punggol

Aircon servicing for all Punggol property types

Punggol is Singapore's youngest and fastest-growing town, with most housing built from 2010 onwards. Blocks here are fitted predominantly with inverter aircons — energy-efficient units designed for the tropics. We cover all Punggol precincts including Waterway, Northshore, Matilda, and Treelodge, with no travel surcharge across the entire planning area.

Living near the Punggol Waterway means higher ambient humidity, which accelerates the buildup of dust, condensation, and algae in aircon drainage pans. Even a unit that's only 2–3 years old can develop drainage blockages or coil deposits that reduce cooling efficiency. We recommend servicing every 3 months for Punggol units, particularly those in waterfront-facing blocks.
