Which parts of Singapore do you serve?+
All of Singapore — East, West, North, Central, and CBD. We're based locally and our team covers the whole island. No travel surcharges.
Do you provide free quotations?+
Yes. Site visits and quotations are always complimentary. We'll come down, assess the scope, and give you an itemised quote within 2 working days — no obligation.
How long does a condo renovation typically take?+
Most condo renovations run 6–10 weeks depending on scope. We'll give you a realistic timeline before we start and keep you updated throughout — no guessing games.
Are you insured?+
Yes, AFIX is fully insured for all works undertaken. We can provide documentation before any work begins. We also handle MCST submission for condo renovations.
Do you take on small repair jobs, or just big projects?+
Both. We handle everything from a single leaking tap to a full building maintenance contract. If it needs fixing, we're happy to help — no job is too small.
