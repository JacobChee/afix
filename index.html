What areas do you serve?+
We operate across Singapore — residential, commercial, and industrial. Just give us a call and we'll confirm quickly.
Do you provide free quotations?+
Yes, all site visits and quotations are complimentary. We assess the scope on-site and provide a detailed itemised quote — no hidden charges, no pressure.
How long does a typical renovation take?+
A standard condo renovation typically runs 6–10 weeks. Commercial fit-outs vary widely. We give you a realistic timeline upfront and stick to it.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes. AFIX holds the relevant BCA contractor licences and is fully insured. We can provide documentation on request before any work begins.
What's included in your facilities maintenance contracts?+
Our contracts cover planned preventive maintenance (PPM), reactive repair works, M&E servicing, and aircon maintenance — tailored to your property.

Fill out the form below or give us a call. We'll arrange a site visit and come back to you with a clear proposal — usually within 2 working days.
